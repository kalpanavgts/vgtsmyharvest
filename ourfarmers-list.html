About Farmer & Farm : Vembu Farm, Located At A 45 Minutes Drive From Heart Of Chennai In A Beautiful Village Called Sembedu, Near Tiruvallur District Is The First Community Farm Of Myharvest Farms. Taken Care By Hard-Working Farmer Named Ellaiah, The Farm Hosts 25 Families And Consists Of Vegetables Chosen By Urban Farmers, From Spinach To Tomatoes. It Also Has A Poultry Farm Nearby Which Is Maintained By Ramkumar, An Engineering Graduate But An Ardent Lover Of Farming. Vembu Farm Has All Arrangements Made For Farm Visits – From Car Parking Space To A Lovely Hut To Rest In A Sunny Day For A Real Farm Experience.
